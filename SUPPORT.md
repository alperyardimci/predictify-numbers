# Support - Predictify Numbers

## How to Play

1. **Select Difficulty**: Choose a digit count between 2 and 5
2. **Make a Guess**: Enter your number using the on-screen keypad
3. **Read the Feedback**:
   - **+X (Bulls)**: X digits are in the correct position
   - **-Y (Cows)**: Y digits exist in the number but are in the wrong position
4. **Keep Guessing**: Use the feedback to narrow down the secret number
5. **Win**: Find the exact number in as few moves as possible!

## Tips & Strategies

- Start with numbers that have all different digits to maximize information
- Pay attention to both Bulls and Cows to eliminate possibilities
- Use process of elimination based on previous guesses
- Try to improve your record time and move count for each difficulty

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How is the secret number generated?
The first digit is always between 1-9 (never starts with 0). Remaining digits can be 0-9, and digits may repeat.

### Q: Where are my records stored?
All records are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any server.

### Q: How do I reset my records?
You can reset your records by reinstalling the app.

### Q: Does the app work offline?
Yes! Predictify Numbers works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

### Q: Is the app free?
Yes, Predictify Numbers is completely free with no in-app purchases or advertisements.
