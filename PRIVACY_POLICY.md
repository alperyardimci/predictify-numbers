# Privacy Policy - Predictify Numbers

**Last Updated: February 5, 2026**

## Introduction

Predictify Numbers ("we", "our", or "the app") is a number guessing game developed by Alper Yardimci. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### We Do NOT Collect Personal Data

Predictify Numbers is designed with your privacy in mind. The app:

- **Does not collect** any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- **Does not require** account registration or login
- **Does not use** any analytics or tracking services
- **Does not contain** advertisements or ad tracking
- **Does not share** any data with third parties
- **Does not access** your camera, microphone, contacts, or location

### Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data **only on your device** using AsyncStorage:

- **Game Records**: Your best scores (number of moves and time) for each difficulty level
- **Game Preferences**: Your selected difficulty level

This data:
- Is stored exclusively on your device
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is never shared with anyone
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Third-Party Services

Predictify Numbers does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to play.

## Children's Privacy

Predictify Numbers does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted over the internet, your information is protected by your device's built-in security measures.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
